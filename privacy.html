Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

OptimaLrn ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Slurp - Japanese Ramen Guide app ("the App"). This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location: Used to display nearby ramen shops and map features. Collected only while the app is in use.
  • Camera & Photo Library: Used for the food ticket scanner and photo upload features.
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition: Used for voice search functionality.
  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and display name.
  • Reviews & Posts: Content you submit is stored on Firebase servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To search and display nearby ramen shops
  • To enable review posting and display
  • To provide food ticket translation and scanning features
  • To improve app quality and user experience
  • To respond to support inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, database, and analytics
  • Google Places API (Google LLC) — Shop information retrieval
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — Login functionality

We do not sell or share your personal information with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored on Firebase servers. To request account deletion, please contact us at support@optimalrnjpn.com.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
