Frequently Asked Questions

Q. The app won't open or keeps crashing.
Please fully close the app and relaunch it. If the issue persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app.
Q. Nearby ramen shops are not showing up.
Please make sure location access is enabled. Go to Settings → Slurp → Location → select "While Using the App".
Q. The food ticket scanner is not working.
Please allow camera access. Go to Settings → Slurp → Camera and enable it.
Q. I can't log in.
Please try signing in again with your Google or Apple account. If the issue continues, contact us below.
Q. I can't post a review.
You must be logged in to post a review. Also, please check your internet connection.

Contact Us

If your issue is not resolved above, please reach out to us. We typically respond within 2–3 business days.
